# PHQ-9 {phq-9}

Over the last 2 weeks, how often have you been bothered by any of the following problems?

## Little interest or pleasure in doing things {little-interest-or-pleasure-in}
(likert categories)
- Not at all
- Several days
- More than half the days
- Nearly every day

## Feeling down, depressed, or hopeless {feeling-down,-depressed,-or-ho}
(likert categories)
- Not at all
- Several days
- More than half the days
- Nearly every day

## Trouble falling or staying asleep, or sleeping too much {trouble-falling-or-staying-asl}
(likert categories)
- Not at all
- Several days
- More than half the days
- Nearly every day

## Feeling tired or having little energy {feeling-tired-or-having-little}
(likert categories)
- Not at all
- Several days
- More than half the days
- Nearly every day

## Poor appetite or overeating {poor-appetite-or-overeating}
(likert categories)
- Not at all
- Several days
- More than half the days
- Nearly every day

## Feeling bad about yourself—or that you are a failure or have let yourself or your family down {feeling-bad-about-yourself—or}
(likert categories)
- Not at all
- Several days
- More than half the days
- Nearly every day

## Trouble concentrating on things, such as reading the newspaper or watching television {trouble-concentrating-on-thing}
(likert categories)
- Not at all
- Several days
- More than half the days
- Nearly every day

## Moving or speaking so slowly that other people could have noticed? Or the opposite—being so fidgety or restless that you have been moving around a lot more than usual {moving-or-speaking-so-slowly-t}
(likert categories)
- Not at all
- Several days
- More than half the days
- Nearly every day

## Thoughts that you would be better off dead or of hurting yourself in some way {thoughts-that-you-would-be-bet}
(likert categories)
- Not at all
- Several days
- More than half the days
- Nearly every day
